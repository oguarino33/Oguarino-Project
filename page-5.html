20 Best Restaurants in Orange County — Top-Rated Places to Eat!

Orange County is the perfect destination for your next food trip. The region in Southern California is home to what food buffs love: a wide range of flavors and varied dining experiences.Just take your pick from the American favorites and dishes with international influences served in the region. For diverse dining experiences, head to the upscale restaurants, cafes, pubs, and steakhouses, among others, that dot the region’s landscape, too.
