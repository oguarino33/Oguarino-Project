About Us

This site is for anyone who loves to discover new food and for everyone who doesn’t. I love to discover and try new food, but I haven’t always. In fact, for most of my early adult life, dinner usually meant microwaving a frozen dinner or adding boiling water to something that came from a box. It wasn’t until I started eating real food while going out with friends and on road trips that I began to discover my passion. I started being the one to find the places to eat and realized that I could really help people. Eating real food was really fun. And gaining confidence as the food finder and business owner? That was life changing!

We can sometimes get lost in the structure and routine of everyday life. Finding new places to eat and expanding my palet has given me a pathway to discovering not only new foods but different cultures as well. With these different foods comes different tradidions, spices, preparation, social dynamics. Our company takes our expertise and the headache out of choosing a place to eat or where to have date night. We find the best spots in town and share them with everyone! Of course this would not be possible without our amazing Team, Amy and Jenna.

Meet the Team

Founder Amy Canto

Amy Canto is the founder of Healthy Food. She began her career in a local bakery where she found her passion for making food and discovering new recipes. She would travel around looking for recipe inspiration and find many amazing small businesses around town. After spending years going around to all these restaurants, she became friends with co-founder, Jenna Ward, and realized they could use this knowledge and share it with the world. Today, she resides in Southern California and enjoys hearing the stories of founders of these restaurants and loves her position here at Healthy Food.

Founder Jenna Ward

Our next founder is Jenna Ward! She is from the East Coast and came to the West Coast to follow her dreams to work with food. She has considered herself a "foody" her whole life, but was never the best cook or baker. Sh, she found joy in testing out her family and friends new recipes. By doing so, she became and expert and would test food and restaurants wherever she went. She loves her career and would not change it for the world!
